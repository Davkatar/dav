About DAV Public School, Katar

Established with a vision to impart quality education rooted in Indian culture and values, DAV Public School, Katar is one of the leading institutions under the DAV College Managing Committee, New Delhi.

The school ensures the intellectual, physical, emotional, and moral growth of every child.

Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a nurturing atmosphere.

Our Vision

To build future leaders grounded in Indian values and global perspective, with a spirit of inquiry, innovation, and compassion.

Our Mission

Providing holistic education that nurtures intellectual, physical, emotional, and spiritual growth of every student.
